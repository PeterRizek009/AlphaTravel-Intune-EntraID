# AlphaTravel – Company Structure

## Overview
- Industry: Travel & Tourism
- Model: 1 Head Office + 5 Branches
- Branches: Sales-only + 1 Branch Manager
- Head Office: Management, IT, Finance/HR, Operations, Developers

## Locations
- Head Office (HO)
- Branch1 (BR1)
- Branch2 (BR2)
- Branch3 (BR3)
- Branch4 (BR4)
- Branch5 (BR5)

## Employees (Example)
### Head Office (35)
- Management: 3
- IT Support: 4
- Developers (macOS): 10
- Finance & HR: 6
- Operations: 12

### Each Branch (11 × 5 = 55)
- Sales: 10
- Branch Manager: 1

Total Users: 90

## Device Strategy
- Developers (HO): macOS (Corporate)
- All other employees: Windows (Corporate)
- Sales mobility: Android (Corporate-owned)
